INSTRUCTIONS

You get a space craft to shoot enemy crafts
Enemy crafts are generated continuously
Two enemy crafts are generated at once
You can move your space craft to left by pressing A
You can move your space craft to right by pressing D
Press space bar to shoot
You have three lives at the beginning
You loose a life when an enemy craft hits your space craft
Game ends when all three lives are over
Press esc to end game whenever necessary


Press any key to go back to the main menu
